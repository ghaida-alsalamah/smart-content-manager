# Smart Content Manager (SCM) — مدير المحتوى الذكي  

Smart Content Manager (SCM) is an AI-powered analytics platform designed primarily for content creators to better understand their performance, plan growth strategies, and determine fair pricing for brand collaborations.

The platform transforms raw social media analytics into clear insights, growth forecasts, and actionable strategy plans. By analyzing creator performance data, SCM helps creators make smarter decisions about their content strategy, posting behavior, and monetization.

Brands can also connect with creators through structured collaboration requests.

---

## Problem

Many content creators rely on intuition when making decisions about:

- posting frequency
- audience growth strategies
- pricing brand collaborations

Without proper analytics tools, creators often struggle to understand performance changes, detect engagement issues, or plan long-term growth strategies.

As a result, many creators rely on guesswork instead of data-driven decisions.

---

## Solution

Smart Content Manager converts creator analytics data into actionable intelligence.

By combining KPI analytics with AI reasoning, the platform helps creators:

- understand engagement patterns
- detect performance risks
- forecast future growth
- generate structured strategy plans
- determine fair advertising pricing

This allows creators to move from intuition-based decisions to data-driven content strategy.

Brands can also send collaboration requests directly through the platform.

---

## Key Features

### For Creators (Core Product)

**CSV Analytics Upload**
Creators can upload social media analytics data using a CSV file. The system automatically detects columns and generates a full analytics dashboard.

**KPI Dashboard**
Automatically calculates key performance metrics including:
- Engagement Rate
- Follower Growth
- Revenue Volatility
- Engagement Quality
- Total Followers
- Total Posts
- Average Monthly Revenue

**Health Score**
A performance score (0–100) combining:
- engagement strength
- audience growth
- revenue stability
- posting consistency

**Interactive Charts**
Visual dashboards showing trends in:
- follower growth
- engagement rate
- posting activity
- revenue performance

**AI Insights**
Claude AI analyzes creator performance data and identifies important patterns such as:
- engagement decline
- audience fatigue
- unstable revenue patterns
- growth opportunities

**AI Strategy Planning**
The platform generates AI-powered growth strategies based on creator performance data. Creators can explore strategy plans for different time horizons:
- 30-day strategy
- 3-month strategy
- 6-month strategy

Each plan includes actionable recommendations for improving engagement, optimizing content strategy, and increasing monetization opportunities.

**Scenario Simulation**
Creators can test what-if scenarios by adjusting posting frequency or engagement rates and observing projected outcomes.

**Ad Pricing Intelligence**
The system calculates recommended pricing tiers for brand collaborations using a multi-factor pricing model. Price tiers include:
- Minimum price
- Ideal price
- Premium price

All pricing recommendations are displayed in Saudi Riyals (﷼ SAR).

**AI Chatbot**
A built-in assistant allows creators to ask questions about their analytics data in natural language.

---

### For Brands

**Creator Search**
Brands can search registered creators on the platform.

**Collaboration Requests**
Brands can send structured collaboration requests including:
- campaign name
- content format
- budget range
- timeline
- campaign message
- additional requirements

**Email Notifications**
Creators receive collaboration requests through email notifications.

## AI Layer

SCM integrates Claude AI as the reasoning layer of the platform.

The AI analyzes creator analytics data and generates:
- performance insights
- risk detection
- growth strategies
- monetization recommendations

Instead of relying only on static dashboards, the platform provides AI-driven strategic guidance tailored to each creator's performance data.

## Development Approach

This project was built using an AI-assisted development workflow.

The platform was developed using Claude Code through a vibe-coding approach, enabling rapid prototyping and implementation.

Generative AI tools were also used to create realistic CSV datasets for testing the analytics engine and validating KPI calculations.

This workflow allowed the team to move from concept to a working AI-powered platform within the hackathon timeframe.
